The Ground and Water Transportation Network

This interactive map is composed of several map layers, which show ground and water transportation networks in Canada. Railway network layer consists of Track Segments, Railway Crossing, Railway Station, Marker Post, Junction and Railway Structure. The road network layer consists of Road Segments and Ferry Connection Segments, Blocked Passage and Toll Point. The Vessel Traffic Routes layer provides routing measures including established (mandatory) direction of traffic flow, recommended direction of traffic flow, separation lines, separation zones, limits of restricted routing measures, limits of routing measures, precautionary areas, archipelagic sea lanes and fairways designated by regulatory authority.
