Provincial Roles in Flood Mapping

Provinces and territories are responsible for undertaking flood mapping, and many other roles related to addressing flood hazards.

Some provinces undertake engineering studies and flood mapping themselves, while others contract flood mapping to private industry. Others delegate authority for flood mapping to other entities such as municipalities and/or conservation authorities.
