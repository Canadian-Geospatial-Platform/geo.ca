Canada's principal producing mines

This map depicts the location of Canada’s principal producing mines for the given reference year. It includes locations for significant metallic, non metallic and industrial mineral mines, significant metallurgical facilities, coal mines, oil sands mines, and oil and gas fields across the country. Also depicted are the locations of top 100 exploration projects and major geological rock formation in Canada.
