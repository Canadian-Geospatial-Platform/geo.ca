Canadian Hydrographic Service Non-Navigational (NONNA-100) Bathymetric Data

Bathymetric data for Canada provides a wealth of geospatial Ocean and water depth data in Canada’s major navigable waterways.

The CHS NONNA-100 Bathymetric Data products represent digital bathymetric sources acquired for, or by, the Canadian Hydrographic Service. They are generalized to a resolution of approximately 100 metres.

PRODUCT USE: The Canadian Hydrographic Service (CHS) NONNA-100 Bathymetric Data products are NOT FOR NAVIGATIONAL USE

PRODUCT USE (continued):

• Under the Charts and Nautical Publications Regulations 1995 made pursuant to the Canada Shipping Act (2001), notwithstanding exceptions, vessels navigating in Canadian waters must carry and use nautical charts and related publications issued by, or on the authority of, the Canadian Hydrographic Service.
• To obtain official navigational products please see: www.charts.gc.ca
• For bathymetry outside of Canada’s jurisdiction, please consult the appropriate Hydrographic Office.

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:

• The CHS NONNA-100 products represent bathymetric data intended for non-navigational use.
• “NONNA” refers to NON-Navigational.
• “100” refers to the approximate resolution (metres) of the data.
• Data in CHS’ NONNA-100 products does not conform to a unified vertical datum.
• The CHS NONNA-100 Bathymetric Data products represent digital bathymetric sources acquired for or by the CHS, generalized to a resolution of approximately 100 metres.
• The CHS NONNA-100 individual product coverage is as follows:

o South of 68°N the products = 1° latitude X 1° longitude (0.001 degrees)
o 68°N-80°N the products = 1° latitude X 2° longitude (0.002 degrees)
o 80°N and north the products = 1° latitude X 4° longitude (0.004 degrees)

• The products north of 80 degrees are currently under construction. An update will be provided once these products are complete.
• There may be data visible on land. These are elevations that were acquired during airborne LiDAR (Light Detection And Ranging) surveys. Elevation values will appear as a positive number when querying the data.
• Please note that gaps in the data may be visible where modern surveys have not yet been conducted.
• The CHS NONNA-100 products will be updated annually and be made available by the last Friday of April of each calendar year.

PRODUCT FORMATS:

• This bathymetry is available for download in both 32-bit GeoTIFF and XYZ formats. These formats can be opened by multiple GIS platforms, where the data (i.e. depth values) may be queried.
• There are detailed instructions available in the dataset record (access the link below) which show the varying ways to view, access and download this data.
