Caribou Ranges

This interactive map shows, caribou ranges throughout Canada. The map allows the user to focus on three of the most criticaly at risk habitats for caribou in canada. The northern British Columbia range, the southern mountain range and the The Atlantic Canada range. Also depicted are four classifications of conservation areas: Terrestrial areas, main refuges, marine protected areas, and terrestrial areas conserved with other measures.
